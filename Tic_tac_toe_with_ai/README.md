# Tic-Tac-Toe-AI
Simple AI to the Tic-Tac-Toe Game:

## 3 modes:
- Player vs. Player (2 - player mode)
- Player vs. AI (1 - player mode)
- AI vs. AI (*for fun*)

MinMax and Alpha-Beta Pruning used
